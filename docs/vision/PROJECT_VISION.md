# Project Vision — The Abode
*Strategic source of truth. Last updated: 2026-04-11*

---

## What THE ABODE is

THE ABODE is a visual operating environment for an AI workforce.

It is a living neighborhood of specialized agents that own real workflows, can improve over time, and are built toward creating economic value or materially multiplying productivity.

The neighborhood UI is the true product surface — not a cosmetic wrapper. Houses represent real specializations with real ownership and real outcomes. Peter is the user-facing front door into that workforce. Governance, orchestration, and operating services run behind him to keep the system safe, auditable, and cost-efficient.

---

## What it is being built toward

Near term: a stable, trustworthy AI workforce where I can watch agents work, understand what is happening, and intervene cleanly when needed.

Mid term: at least one house creates real economic value or clearly covers its operating costs. Coordination becomes less manual.

Long term: multiple houses create net value. Proven houses can be duplicated or productized. Parts of the platform or individual houses can be sold separately. The Abode operates as a real autonomous business ecosystem.

---

## What it is not

- Not a developer console or raw dashboard
- Not a fake simulation with theater substituting for real performance
- Not a flat list of scripts that happen to call LLMs
- Not a system where every subsystem is a named public-facing agent

---

## The core user experience

- I talk to Peter
- I watch Belfort trade and improve (improvement today means operator-approved strategy updates, not autonomous adaptation)
- I understand what is happening without reading code
- I act on important decisions without digging through technical clutter
- The neighborhood expands as the workforce matures — visuals reflect real state, not aspirational theater
